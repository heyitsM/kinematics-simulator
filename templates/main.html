Welcome to A Physics and Math Simulator!

Hello. This is A Physics and Math Simulator, a website that does quick highschool physics and middle-high school math level calculations and implements graphical representations of them. In the future, I am looking to implement more simulation/calculator options! Feel free to let me know if you have any suggestions, by heading over to the repository and opening an issue.
